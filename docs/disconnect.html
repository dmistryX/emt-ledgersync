Two safe ways to revoke local and remote access

Disconnect In A Controlled Way

You can disconnect from QuickBooks Online and also clear local app data used by the desktop client.

Method 1: QuickBooks Online (Recommended)

This revokes app access directly from your QuickBooks account.

  1. Log into QuickBooks Online.
  2. Open Settings in the top-right menu.
  3. Go to Apps then Manage apps.
  4. Find Emendate LedgerSync.
  5. Click Disconnect and confirm.

Method 2: Remove Local App Data

Use this to force local sign-out/reset on your desktop app.

  1. Close Emendate LedgerSync.
  2. Open %APPDATA%\<install-folder-name>\.
  3. Delete Emendate_LedgerSync.sqlite3.
  4. If present, also delete Emendate_LedgerSync.sqlite3-shm and Emendate_LedgerSync.sqlite3-wal.
  5. Reopen the application.

Complete Removal Checklist

  1. Disconnect from QuickBooks (Method 1).
  2. Uninstall the desktop app.
  3. Remove local files from %APPDATA%\<install-folder-name>\ if you want a full cleanup:
  • Emendate_LedgerSync.sqlite3
  • Emendate_LedgerSync.sqlite3-shm
  • Emendate_LedgerSync.sqlite3-wal
  • Emendate_LedgerSync.log
Note: Disconnecting removes app access to QuickBooks. It does not delete records already imported into QuickBooks.
