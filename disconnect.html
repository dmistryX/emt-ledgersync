How to Disconnect QuickBooks Bill Importer

You can disconnect the application from your QuickBooks account using either method below:

Method 1: Via QuickBooks Online (Recommended)

  1. Log into your QuickBooks Online account
  2. Click the ⚙ Settings icon in the top right
  3. Select Apps from the menu
  4. Click Manage apps
  5. Find QuickBooks Bill Importer in the list
  6. Click Disconnect
  7. Confirm the disconnection

Result: The application will no longer have access to your QuickBooks data.

Method 2: Delete Local Token File

  1. Navigate to the application folder on your computer
  2. Find the file named: qbo_tokens.json
  3. Delete this file
  4. Restart the application

Result: The application will show as disconnected and require re-authentication.

Complete Removal

To completely remove the application:

  1. Disconnect using Method 1 above
  2. Delete the application folder from your computer
  3. Delete these files if they exist:
    • config.json
    • qbo_tokens.json
    • quickbooks_importer.log
Note: After disconnecting, the application will no longer be able to access your QuickBooks data. Your Excel files and any data stored locally remain on your computer.
